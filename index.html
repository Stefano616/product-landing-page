About

PDMS (polydimethilsyloxane) is widely used in research. Here are presented some advantages and its disadvantages.

Pros

  • Optically transparent
  • Flexible
  • Not expensive

Cons

  • Hidrophobic
  • Small molecules absorption

Pricing

Single channel

A single piece of PDMS with one straight circular channel embedded in it. The best choice for setting up new assays.

5 $ / piece

Multiple channels

From two up to 8 parallel straight channels are embedded in one single slab of PDMS. Optimal for technical repetitions for statistical purpose, or for experiments with multiple conditions.

10-30 $ / piece

Custom design

Your own design can be implemented in a cad or L-Edit file, in order to produce costum PDMS devices. The way to go if you already have your working device.

Price varying on the complexity
